Hi, I am a sleepless UX Designer in Seattle. I thrive to create simple and consistent experiences that can empower both the business and the user. I am an interdisciplinary problem solver with deep empathy for users from different backgrounds.

Born in Madison, Wisconsin, I grew up in Seoul, South Korea and Tokyo, Japan. After studying digital art and computer science at Wellesley College, I moved to Seattle to learn more about UX design.

In my spare time, you can find me experimenting with AR/VR, painting, watching documentaries, and trying out new Trader Joe’s snacks and restaurants.
