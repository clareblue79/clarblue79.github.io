Hi, I am a UX Designer in Seattle. I thrive to create simple and consistent experiences that can empower both the business and the user.

Born in Madison, Wisconsin, I grew up in Seoul, South Korea and Tokyo, Japan. After graduating Wellesley College with a B.A in "Media Arts and Sciences (Digital Art + Computer Science)," I moved to Seattle three months ago because I fell in love with UX. Thanks to my background, I am an interdisciplinary problem solver with deep empathy for users from different backgrounds.
